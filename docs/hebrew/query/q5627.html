Description

Lam 3:13 mentions בְּנֵי אַשְׁפָּתוֹ (lit. sons of his quiver) to mean arrows. There is a similar expression in Job where בֶן-קָשֶׁת (lit. son of a bow) also refers to the arrow. HALOT identifies both instances as metaphorical. Thus, this query searches for the occurrences of בֶן-קָשֶׁת in the HB, which turns out to be only used in Job 41:20.
