Description

A query motivated by the LXX reading of Lev 25:16: ὅτι ἀριθμὸν γενημάτων αὐτοῦ οὕτως ἀποδώσεταί σοι. Where γενημάτων αὐτοῦ appears to read תבואת הוא as a construct phrase. Wevers interprets the Greek as a mistake rather than an alternate reading of the Hebrew (Notes on the Greek Text of Leviticus, 411). Is this pattern attested in the HB?
