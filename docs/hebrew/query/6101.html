QUESTION 3 ESSAY 0913

In 1 Sam 16:14, we read about “an evil spirit from God”. Is this reference unique, or are there other references to an evil spirit of God/the Lord”?

A few simple notes on the query:

  1. Since the adjective can be placed before or after the noun and within the "UNORDEREDGROUP" operator, only ‘bare’ object blocks can be used (no ‘..’ or OR operators), I did not use this operator.

  2. Since between the noun and the corresponding Adjective, there can potentially be (independently of who comes first) one or more words, I inserted the ".." operator in both cases.

  3. Since between "spirit" and "God"/"YHWH", there can potentially be one or more words, I inserted the ".." operator.
