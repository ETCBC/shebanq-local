Description

This query searches for all phrases with (in any order, hence the [UnorderedGroup) the words ‘evil’, ‘spirit’, and ‘God/YHWH/Adonai’.

I have not specified whether ‘evil’ should be an adjective or a noun because ‘evil’ could be attributive to the noun ‘spirit’ in both forms. I have chosen to search for phrases instead of sentences, as we are searching for the phrase ‘an evil spirit of God/the Lord’. When running with [sentence instead of [phrase, the query detects another occurrence, namely Judges 9:23, where God (as the grammatical subject) sends an evil spirit.
