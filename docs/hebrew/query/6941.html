Description

This query searches for those places in the OT that talk about Aaron's staff. This yields 4 results. The search is done in two ways. First by looking for the word combination of 'staff' and 'Aaron'. Then by looking for those phrases within which 'staff' and 'Aaron' occur, where these words are dependent on each other. As a proper noun, the word Aaron is dependent on the word Staff as a noun.
