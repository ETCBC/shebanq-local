Description

Search for evil spirits of God: I did this by searching for: 1. phrases with: 1a. RWX RLHJM, JHWH or >L (this can conceivably also give results of phrases where the spirit is not 'of' or 'coming from' God, but they have to be picked out by hand - better this than missing relevant results by forcing 'evil spirit' and 'God' to be strictly adjacent), or 1b. RWX >LHJM RH". So if you really want to make sure you have all of them, it might be better to just find all phrases with RWX .. R>H and then manually sort them out based on whether God is involved... And of course one could insert additional possible designations of 'God' if desired.

Query instruction (mql)

select all objects where

[phrase FOCUS
   [word lex="RWX/"]
   [word lex0="R<"]
   ..
   [word (lex=">LHJM/" OR lex="JHWH/" OR lex=">L/")]
   OR
   [word lex="RWX/"]
   [word (lex=">LHJM/" OR lex="JHWH/" OR lex=">L/")]
   [word lex0="R<"]
]
OR
[clause FOCUS
   [UnorderedGroup
      [phrase function IN (Subj,Objc)
         [word FIRST lex=">LHJM/" OR lex="JHWH/" OR lex=">L/"]
      ]
      [phrase function IN (Objc, Subj)
         [word lex="RWX/"]
         [word lex0="R<"]
      ]
   ]
]
