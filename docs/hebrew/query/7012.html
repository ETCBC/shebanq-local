Description

When I searched through another query (http://shebanq.ancient-data.org/hebrew/query?version=2021&id=6919) for the direct claim of the people of Israel to God, I noticed that God understands complaining as speaking to Him. Therefore, in this query, I look for the combination 'murmuring' and 'against Me' in the book of Numbers, to find out when God indicates that the people were murmuring against Him.
