Description

Usually when speaking about blessing (Hebrew: ברך) people think of an act by God towards people, but can God also be the object of this verb? Where? This query looks for all common cases where God is the object of the verb ברך. The different names of God are: יהוה ,אדני and אלהים. The predicate could be placed before or after the object. I wrote all my queries in two ways (before and after the object) for each of the three names of God.
