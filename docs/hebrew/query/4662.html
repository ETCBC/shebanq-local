Description

Search for instances of "Elohim JHWH" as a designation of God in the OT. I excluded phrases with ">L >LHJM JHWH" (since it means "God of gods, JHWH") and "H>LHJM JHWH" (since it means "the god JHWH"). This gives Ps 68:27 as first and only result. The same happens if one forces the object to be just Elohim JHWH, by typing FIRST after the first word and LAST after the last. Without these constraints, the first occurrence is Josua 22:22 (2x, but it is 'God of gods, JHWH)
