Description

This query looks in the Hebrew Bible for all locational phrases within quotation consisting of:
• a subphrase containing one of the nouns מקום 'place', ארץ 'land',
  עיר 'city' and בית 'house', directly followed by
• a subphrase (daughter of the first) with a demonstrative
  relation to its mother.

Query instruction (mql)

Select all objects where

[clause txt~"Q"
   [phrase
      [subphrase AS s1 FOCUS
         [word lex='MQWM/' OR lex='>RY/' 
          OR lex='<JR/' OR lex='BJT/']
      ]
      [subphrase FOCUS rela=dem AND
       mother HAS s1.self]
   ]
]
