Description

In 1 Sam 16:14 we read about “an evil spirit from God”. Is this reference unique, or are there other references of an evil spirit of God/the Lord? The word רֽוּחַ is a noun and could be a part of a status-constructus. The different names of God are: יהוה ,אדני and אלהים. The name of God can be connected to רֽוּחַ with a status-constructus or with a preposition.
