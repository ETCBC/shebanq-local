Description

This query looks for the combination of two of the most occurring designations of God (JHWH and >LHJM), regardless of their order. The query only finds them when they are adjacent and part of the same clause. The latter restriction excludes cases that look like this: "...said the Lord. God did according to..." The first occurrence is Gen. 2:4, at the beginning of the second creation narrative.
