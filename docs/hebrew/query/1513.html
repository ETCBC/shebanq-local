Description

Looking for all clauses in the Psalms containing a 1s form of אמר and a complement containing the preposition –ל. The purpose of this query is to find parallels to the construction in Ps 16:2 and to evaluated the suggested emendation of אָמַרְתְּ (2fs) into אָמַרְתִּי (1s) in this verse.

For further details, see Wido van Peursen, "Patterns and Pleasure: Participants in Psalm 16", Jaap Dekker and Geert Kwakkel (eds.), Reading and Listening. Meeting One God in Many Texts. Festschrift for Eric Peels on the Occasion of his 25th Jubilee as Professor of Old Testament Studies (Amsterdamse Cahiers voor Exegese van de Bijbel en zijn Tradities, Supplement Series 16; Bergambacht: 2VM, 2018), pp. 179-187

Query instruction (mql)

Select all objects where

[book book = Psalmi
   [clause
      [phrase typ = VP
        [word focus lex = ">MR[" AND ps = p1 AND nu = sg]
      ]
      [phrase function = Cmpl
        [word focus lex = "L"]
      ]
   ]

OR

[clause
      [phrase function = Cmpl
        [word focus lex = "L"]
      ]
      [phrase typ = VP
        [word focus lex = ">MR[" AND ps = p1 AND nu = sg]
      ]
   ]
