Description

Looking for 1s forms of אמר in the Psalms introducing direct speech, followed the 2ms pronoun אַתָּה in the first clause of that direct speech. The purpose of this query is to find parallels to the construction in Ps 16:2, where an emendation from אָמַרְתְּ (2fs) into אָמַרְתִּי (1s) has been suggested.

For further details, see Wido van Peursen, “Patterns and Pleasure: Participants in Psalm 16", Jaap Dekker and Geert Kwakkel (eds.), Reading and Listening. Meeting One God in Many Texts. Festschrift for Eric Peels on the Occasion of his 25th Jubilee as Professor of Old Testament Studies (Amsterdamse Cahiers voor Exegese van de Bijbel en zijn Tradities, Supplement Series 16; Bergambacht: 2VM, 2018), pp. 179-187
