Description

A search to determine whether the phrase "The word of which it is to say it happened..." consistently denotes the beginning of a new unit. The section query looks at a similar phrase, "It happened the word of YHWH to Jeremiah," which only sometimes functions as the beginning of a new unit.

[book book IN (Jeremia)
 [word FOCUS lex = "H"]

 [word FOCUS lex = ">CR"]
